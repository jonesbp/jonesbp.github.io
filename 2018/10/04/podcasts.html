10 Podcasts You Might Not Listen To

04 Oct 2018

  • Afropop Worldwide: A long-tenured public radio show on music from Africa and across the African diaspora
  • Coin Talk: An irreverent weekly chat between Aaron Lammer and Jay Kang (and sometimes guests) on goings on in the cryptocurrency world
  • Dank Rares: Interviews with people exploring the possibilities of blockchain technology for market-making in digital art
  • Food Safety Talk: Commentary on recent news in food safety policy and research combined with shaggy, rambling chat about an array of other subjects between two food safety academics
  • GAMETHING: Small collages of sound, voiceover, and music created in response to video game experiences
  • Gravy: Tightly produced programs on food and culture topics in the U.S. South from the Southern Foodways Alliance
  • Hobby Horse: Matt Haughey interviews people about a personal interest or hobby outside of their field of expertise
  • Too Much Not Enough: Chats between Emma Winston and Darius Kazemi in which they take turns introducing each other to topics of obsessive interest
  • Track Changes: Chats between Postlight agency founders Paul Ford and Rich Ziade (and frequently guests) on topics in business and technology
  • Twenty Thousand Hertz: Like a 99% Invisible for sound design
